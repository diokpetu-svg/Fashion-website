The Problem

Reasons you're
losing customers
right now

Most business owners don't realise their website is actively costing them. Potential customers visit, find it lacking, and quietly leave for a competitor — without saying a word.

🕰️
Your website looks outdated
First impressions are made in under a second. An old, cluttered site signals an old, unprofessional business — before a customer reads a single word.
📱
It's painful on mobile
Over 70% of visitors will be on their phone. If your site is hard to use, hard to read, or slow to load on mobile, they leave immediately.
🐌
Slow loading pages
53% of users abandon a site that takes more than 3 seconds to load. A slow website doesn't just frustrate — it disappears in search rankings too.
🔗
Impossible to contact you
If a customer has to search to find your phone number, or your contact form is broken, they won't try again. That enquiry goes to your competitor instead.
🏆
Competitors look more professional
When customers compare you side by side with competitors online, your website IS your business. Looking less polished means losing the deal before it starts.

How much does a website cost? +
Pricing depends on the scope of your project — a simple landing page is significantly different from a full multi-page business website. I'll always give you a clear, fixed quote after our discovery call, so there are no surprises. Most business websites fall within a range that offers serious return on investment from the first few customers it generates.
How long does it take to build? +
Most projects are completed and live within 7–14 days from our first call. The speed depends on how quickly you can provide your content (logo, photos, text) and how many rounds of revisions are needed. I work in focused sprints specifically to avoid the months-long delays that frustrate most business owners.
What about hosting and domain? +
I can handle everything — domain registration, hosting setup, SSL certificates, and connecting it all together. Alternatively, if you already have hosting or a domain, I'll work with what you have. Ongoing hosting costs are separate and straightforward — I'll explain all options clearly before we start.
Can I update the website myself after it launches? +
Yes. Depending on how we build your site, you'll either have access to a content management system where you can edit text, images, and pages without any coding knowledge, or I'll handle updates for you as part of a maintenance arrangement. We'll discuss what suits you best before building.
Is the website mobile friendly? +
Always. Every website I build is designed mobile-first — meaning I design for phones first, then scale up to desktop. This is the correct approach given that over 70% of your website visitors are likely on a mobile device. You'll never get a site from me that doesn't work perfectly on mobile.
What if I already have a website? +
A redesign is often a bigger transformation than starting from scratch. I'll review your current site, identify exactly what's holding back your results, and rebuild with those problems solved. You keep your domain and any content worth keeping — we just make it dramatically better.
What happens after the website launches? +
You'll have 30 days of free support after launch to fix any issues, make minor tweaks, and ask any questions. After that, I offer optional maintenance packages for businesses who want ongoing updates, performance monitoring, and priority support. Most clients continue working with me on an ongoing basis.
